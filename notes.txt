Django-Grocery-POS
functionalities of this system:
1.) Serve as an inventory system
2.) Enables empoyees to add and edit products (only superuser can delete)
3.) Have an account for each employee, with information
4.) Employees can start a transaction, and every transaction can be ascribed to him
5.) Customer model
6.) For each transaction, it will be linked to an employee (NOT NULL)
                          and to a customer (if customer chooses so)
